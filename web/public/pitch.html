Separate the vibe from the hard rules.

02Hard filter

Drop wrong medium, unverified artists, prices over 1.5× budget.

03Top 60 by meaning

Every work has a meaning fingerprint. Pick the 60 closest — that's how "lo-fi" finds "chill."

04Score the 60

Apply the objective function (below). One composite score per candidate.

05Round-robin

Spread picks across artists. No one creator dominates.

06Quota + confidence

Guarantee ≥ 2 emerging in top 8. Tag each result HIGH / MED / LOW.

↘ the objective function
score  =  1.0 · relevance  +  0.5 · emerging‑boost  −  0.6 · price‑penalty
1.0 relevance meaning-match score (0–1)
0.5 emerging boost extra credit for under-followed artists — our mission, in code
0.6 price penalty soft — zero at budget, maxes out at 1.5×

05 — the decisions three trade-offs
05Three calls we made — each with a cost
01

Every artist and every work is verified.

Onboarding takes longer. Supply grows slower.

02

Price is a soft penalty, not a hard wall.

Strict-budget buyers may see slight overages.

03

We lean hard into emerging-artist exposure.

A less-perfect emerging sometimes beats a perfect-fit established. That's the mission.
